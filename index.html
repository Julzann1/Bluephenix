About Us

Blue Phenix Skills Training Center Corporation (BPSTC) is a TESDA- accredited training center located in Baguio City. The school aims to provide technical education skills to Filipinos and contribute to nation-building, particularly by producing individuals with excellent industrial skills. BPSTC offers Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) programs with short courses under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) which equips the students with technical skills. Aside from this, they can also earn a National Certificate (NC) which they can use for employment.

FEATURED COURSES

Web Development NC III

The Web Development NC III Qualification consists of competencies that a person must achieve to develop websites for hosting via intranet or Internet. It includes competencies for web design, content development, client-side/server-side scripting and website security configuration, among other tasks.

Computer Systems Servicing NC II

The COMPUTER SYSTEMS SERVICING NC II Qualification consists of competencies that must possess to enable to install and configure computers systems, set-up computer networks and servers and to maintain and repair computer systems and networks.

Cookery NC II

The TESDA Course in COOKERY NC II consist of competencies that a perosn must achieve to clean kitchen areas, prepare hot, cold meals and desserts for guest in various foood and beverage service facilities

TESDA Baguio-Benguet kicks off 2022 Provincial Skills Competition

BAGUIO CITY-The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) Benguet commenced the 2022 Provincial Skills Competition today, at the Baguio City School of Arts and Trades (BCSAT). A total of 18 competitors and experts from different TESDA Technology Institutions (TTIs) and Technical Vocational Institutions (TVIs) in Baguio-Benguet will showcase their skills in different trade areas.
