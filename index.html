About Us

Blue Phenix Skills Training Center Corporation (BPSTC) is a TESDA- accredited training center located in Baguio City. The school aims to provide technical education skills to Filipinos and contribute to nation-building, particularly by producing individuals with excellent industrial skills. BPSTC offers Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) programs with short courses under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) which equips the students with technical skills. Aside from this, they can also earn a National Certificate (NC) which they can use for employment.

Video Editor

A video editor is involved in video production and the post-production of film making. The video editor's responsibilities involve decisions about the selection and combining of shots into sequences, as well as the addition of accompanying sound effects and music—to ultimately create a finished movie, television program, commercial, promo, or snipe. Video editors usually use non-linear editing software to accomplish the task of editing. A video editor is a technically inclined individual that makes creative video editing decisions.
